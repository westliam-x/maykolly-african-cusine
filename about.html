About us

Founded in 2012 by a Nigerian Mary Emeifonwu, our establishment has been serving up rich African food sourced from various regions of Africa. We are dedicated to bringing Africa to you as best as we can, searching for the best Africa has to offer, and bringing it back to you here in our Restaurant.

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our delightful cuisines the moment you walk inside. Join us, for an outing with friends, a party, or simply just to have a piece of Africa, we have you covered.
